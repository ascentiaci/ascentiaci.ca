Computer System Requirements

To participate in these courses remotely, students must use a modern Windows 11 computer. MacOS and Chromebooks are not supported, and students using these devices will be unable to participate. Below are the basic system requirements:

Requirement Details
Operating System Microsoft Windows 11
Display 14-inch or larger
Webcam Required for exams
Headset with Microphone Recommended for optimal participation
External Mouse A laptop touchpad will work, but it's not ideal
Microsoft Office Office 2021 or 365 (free student license provided)

Most computers that meet or exceed these requirements can be purchased from major retailers for between $500 and $900, before taxes and optional addons.
