Types of Financial Aid

Grants

Grants are typically need-based and may be available through government programs. These do not require repayment. Please check the eligibility criteria and application process for any grants you may be eligible for.

Student Loans

Student loans are a common way to finance your education. Our admissions advisors can guide you through the process of applying for federal and provincial student loans, as well as private loan options. Understand the terms and conditions, and find the best loan options to suit your needs.

Interest-Free Payment Plans

We offer flexible interest-free payment plans to help you manage the cost of your education. These plans allow you to spread out your tuition payments over the course of your program with no interest. Contact our admissions advisors to discuss payment plan options.
